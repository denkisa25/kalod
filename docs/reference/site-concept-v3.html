about

kaloyan dimitrov is a composer, sound designer and mix engineer working across film, television and advertising. from sundance-selected features to full network rebrands, his work lives where picture ends and feeling begins.

two decades of cues for audi, pepsi, nestlé, societe generale, btv and the films of miramar — composed, designed and mixed in sofia, heard everywhere.
